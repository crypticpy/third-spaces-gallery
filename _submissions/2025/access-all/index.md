We believe every kid should be able to find and enjoy third spaces, no matter their abilities or background. Access All is designed from the ground up to be inclusive.

## Accessibility Features

### For Visual Impairments

- Full screen reader support with detailed descriptions
- High contrast mode with customizable colors
- Large text options and dyslexia-friendly fonts
- Audio descriptions of places and routes

### For Mobility Needs

- Wheelchair accessibility ratings for every location
- Accessible route planning (ramps, elevators, flat paths)
- Real-time info on elevator outages or construction
- Sensory-friendly environment indicators

### For Different Languages

- Support for Spanish, Vietnamese, Chinese, and more
- Easy language switching
- Translated reviews from other users
- Audio guides in multiple languages

## Why Accessibility Matters

About 1 in 4 people have some kind of disability. And lots of Austin families speak languages other than English at home. If Third Spaces is going to help ALL Austin youth, it needs to work for everyone.

## Special Features

1. **Accessibility Verified**: Places earn badges for meeting accessibility standards
2. **Community Reports**: Users can report accessibility issues or improvements
3. **Buddy System**: Connect with others who have similar accessibility needs
4. **Quiet Hours**: Find times when places are less crowded and quieter
