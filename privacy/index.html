Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Overview

The Third Spaces Youth Design Gallery is operated by the City of Austin's Resident Data Impact Project (RIDP). We are committed to protecting the privacy of all visitors, especially youth users.

Information We Collect

Voting Data

When you vote on designs, we collect:

  • Vote selections - Which designs you voted for and in which categories
  • Browser fingerprint - A hash of your browser characteristics (not personally identifiable) used only to prevent duplicate votes
  • Timestamp - When you voted

This data is stored locally in your browser and optionally synced to our servers for vote aggregation. We do not collect your name, email, or any personal contact information through voting.

Feedback & Comments

If you leave feedback or comments, we collect the content you submit. Comments are powered by GitHub Discussions and require a GitHub account. GitHub's privacy policy applies to comment data.

Design Submissions

If you submit a design, we collect the information you provide in the submission form, including your first name, school, and grade level. This information will be displayed publicly alongside your design.

How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Display aggregate vote counts on designs
  • Prevent voting manipulation and spam
  • Improve the Third Spaces app based on community feedback
  • Showcase youth designs and give credit to designers

Data Storage & Security

Vote data is stored locally in your browser using localStorage and cookies. If server-side aggregation is enabled, vote data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption.

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your information to third parties, except as required by law.

Children's Privacy

This site is designed for use by Austin youth. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. The voting system is designed to work without any personal information.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information to us, please contact us so we can delete it.

Cookies

We use cookies for:

  • Vote persistence - Remembering your votes if localStorage is cleared
  • Spam prevention - Preventing duplicate voting

We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Clear your vote data by clearing your browser's cookies and localStorage
  • Request removal of your design submission
  • Request information about what data we have collected

Third-Party Services

This site uses the following third-party services:

  • GitHub Pages - For hosting (GitHub Privacy Policy applies)
  • Giscus/GitHub Discussions - For comments (GitHub Privacy Policy applies)
  • Google Fonts - For typography (Google Privacy Policy applies)

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page.

Contact Us
